NEW ERA: Versatile and
Safe Automotive Pump
Solutions

NEW ERA has established itself as a leader in the automotive sector by providing high-quality pumps for a wide range of applications. Our pumps are ideal for spraying release agents, pumping liquefied gases, distributing paints and solvents in centralized systems. Additionally, they can be used for transferring radiator coolant (tropicalizing liquids), pumping fuel and fuel additives.

Built on Trust & Innovation

Founded in 1956 by Mr.S.M.Desai, New Era Group has grown to be a leading pump specialist company in the southern India today. An expert in pumping technology, the company is one of the top few pump distributors in India that distributes reputable and leading brands and has reacted to high demand for trendsetting pumps and systems with a carefully crafted growth strategy.
