CHEMICAL METALLIC PROCESS
PUMP DESCRIPTION

Chemical metallic pumps are designed to allow the highest interchangeability of their parts. Therefore, a small number of spare parts is necessary even when many different pumps are installed in the same plant. Characteristics chemical metallic pumps are designed for diathermic and molten fluids up to 800 °c and is available both in air or water cooled versions.

Maximum Flow Rate 2000 lpm

Maximum Discharge Head or Pressure 76 mwc

Maximum Suction Lift Wet 4 mwc with Foot Valve

Maximum Temperature 600 Deg.C.
