AIR OPERATED DIAPHRAGM
PUMP DESCRIPTION

Air-operated double diaphragm pumps are ideal for pumping liquids with abrasive, corrosive, explosive, and high apparent viscosity, even if containing suspended solids. The stall-prevention circuit guarantees a safe operation without requiring lubricated air. These pumps have achieved unprecedented levels of versatility due to their dry self-priming, no mechanical seals hence no leakage, variable flow and pressure, completely portable, can run dry without damage, no rotating parts less wear and maintenance, does not generate heat, no complex controls required, ideal for abrasive, corrosive, shear sensitive and viscous fluids, intrinsically safe, no efficiency loss due to wear and tear or by changing pressure and flow.

Maximum Flow Rate 800 Lpm

Maximum Discharge Head or Pressure 8 Bar

Maximum Suction Lift Dry or Wet 7mwc

Maximum Solid Passage Size 50 mm

Maximum Temperature 95 Deg.C.

Maximum 40,000 cps
